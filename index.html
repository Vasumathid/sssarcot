Our Story

Shaping Futures in Vellore District

SSS College, located on Bypass Road, Arcot, is a premier Arts & Science institution committed to producing graduates who are ready for the demands of a globalized world. Affiliated to Thiruvalluvar University, our college combines academic excellence with holistic student development.

We have a team of highly qualified and experienced staff who are dedicated in producing outstanding results in University examinations and devoted to the welfare of every student who walks through our doors.

Admission Requirements

  • Original T.C., Marksheets & Community Certificate (2 Xerox copies)
  • 2 recently taken passport-size photographs
  • ₹1,500 UG / ₹2,000 PG University + Special fees (paid once at admission)
  • SC, ST, SCC, Farmer, Beedi Worker, Minority, Pudumai Pen & Tamil Pudhalvan scholarships available
  • Ragging strictly prohibited inside and outside campus
  • Mobile phones not permitted inside college premises or bus
